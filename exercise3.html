Dug the Dog

Dug the dog is a golden retriever that appeared in the 2009 Disney-Pixar movie Up. He used to be owned by a man name Charles Muntz, who gave him a collar that allowed him to speak. While on a mission to find Kevin the bird, Dug meets Carl and Russell and becomes their friend. He eventually goes on the be adopted by Carl. Dug is very sweet, friendly, and silly. He loves balls and squirrels but cane become distracted very easily.
